About DRS Marine Services

DRS Marine, established in 2002, is dedicated to upholding the highest safety standards for both shipowners and our seafarers. Our core mission is the prevention of accidental loss, environmental protection, and strict compliance with all international and company regulations. We believe that proactive, continuous training is the foundation of our world-class safety management system.

We focus intensely on developing our people through specialized training, creating an environment where every crew member achieves their highest professional potential and masters safety at sea.

Oasis Maritime Education

Mastering the Shift to Proactive Risk Mitigation

Driven by Dual Expertise: Founded by a seasoned ship captain with extensive years of experience at sea and proven expertise in training mariners ashore, Oasis Maritime Education understands that true safety is a mindset. Our mission is to move every seafarer beyond the daily routine to a state of daily safety vigilance.
Training Beyond Competence: We deliver highly curated training material that goes beyond mandated requirements. This content is engineered to instill the rigor, discipline, and proactive risk mitigation skills essential for professional excellence and a lifetime of safe service.

Safety, Quality and Training

Our operations are governed by the International Safety Management (ISM) Company Quality Safety Management Systems (QSMS) Codes, ensuring absolute regulatory compliance and a culture of continuous improvement fleet-wide.

We invest in continuous, practical Shipboard Training to guarantee that our crew is not only competent but proactively prepared. This commitment to highly trained personnel translates directly into maximum safety, efficiency, and operational integrity for every voyage.
