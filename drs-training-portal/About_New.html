Our Vision: Enhance employment opportunity for seafarers

ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

Bajwa Maritime Training Institute (BMTI) is built on the real-world insight of a highly experienced group of maritime professionals, backed by over thirty years of expertise in the dynamic shipping sector. This deep understanding of ship management demands and the intricacies of professional performance at sea ensures our ability to consistently elevate professional standards across the maritime workforce.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated team of experienced faculty and instructors translate this wealth of practical knowledge into a rigorous training environment, effectively preparing every trainee to confidently meet and exceed the industry's evolving operational challenges.

The core of our educational offerings includes comprehensive, ISO-standard courses designed to serve both novice and seasoned seafarers. These programs emphasize structured, hands-on learning in vital technical skills, such as welding, lathe machining, and major engine room machinery maintenance. Furthermore, we provide rigorous refresher training focused on life-saving and firefighting appliances, ensuring trainees achieve unwavering confidence in emergency response and essential onboard maintenance procedures.

Finally, our specialized "Bridging Courses" for Deck and Engine Officers are explicitly structured to narrow the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, ensuring a seamless and effective transition into their new rank responsibilities.
